PNL

ItemTotal PNLOur End (50%)SettledAvailableSalMatt
House split: Highland Hill Capital 50% of book PNL and 50% of legal (global). Kickbacks 100% ours, distributed once after funding. FF is full book-level cost on a 4-month lag. Legal total is entered on the Monthly tab. A funding month pays out as soon as it's positive; a negative month is held — no payout, no deduction — until the whole cohort goes dark (every open deal DEFAULT or 30+ days with no payment), then its loss deducts. After a loss is settled, later collections surface as ↩ RECOVERY income to pay back out. The Settled column is a running account.
